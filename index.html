About

I'm an experimented animator and visual designer who loves building things with code.
I studied art and design and after years of working as a designer, I decided to learn animation by myself and I have been working as an animator and motion visual designer for the last 8 years.
My first steps in programming were building small webs with Flash and ActionScript many years ago (who remembers Flash?). After that, working as a motion grapher, I realized that code can make easier my job, so I started to learn javascript and make little scripts to work in after effects.
Soon, I feel that I needed to know more about programming and how was the front-end evolution since my years designing webs. So, I started studying React by myself, and I learned a lot about web development.
A few months later, I was ready for the back-end, so I decided to join a Web Dev Full-Stack Bootcamp at Ironhack. They were 9 intense weeks where I have grown as a developer, working daily in teams, in a realistic environment and dealing with impossible deadlines.
Now, I'm seeking a full-time front-end role where I can contribute with all my skills in coding, design and animation to help a company achieve their goals.
